Fried Egg with Spinat

Description

This is a easy dish, which could be finished around 10 mins. Yet, it is cheap, delicious and nutritious.

Ingredients

  • 500g Spinat
  • 4 Eggs
  • Oil, Salt, MSG, Sugar

Process

  1. Beat the eggs, mix with few salt.
  2. Rinse the Spinat.
  3. Turn on fire, add enough oil, after oil got hot, fried the eggs.
  4. Take out the eggs into a dish for future use.
  5. Turn on the fire again, add few oil, and add Spinat.
  6. Wait until spinat becomes small and water comes out of it. Add fried eggs.
  7. Add seasoning, 1 tsp of salt, 1 tsp of MSG, haft tsp of Suger.
  8. Put into a dish and finished.
